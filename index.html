About Me

My family has been always by my side in ups and downs. They have taught me how to be a better person. My family consists of me, my husband and my daughter.

Within each family member, lies my strength. My daughter is very dependent to me but she is my strength. As she always count on me when I need a shoulder to cry on. She believes in me more than any other person. She is the backbone of our family. My father is someone who will always hide away his troubles for the sake of his family.
