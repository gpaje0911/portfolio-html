About Me

My Family consists of six members. My mom, dad, and 4 siblings, and me as the 3rd child. They used to say that the third child becomes the class clown and is usually a good manipulator but not for me. In my case, I am the most elusive type of person as I didn’t get much attention than my oldest and youngest siblings. That’s why I grew up being an independent person.

Now, I have my own family. I live separately and happily with my supportive husband and lovely daughter who’s my everyday inspiration. I thank and praise God for this God-centered family. He built this family and I will never trade it for any other good thing in this world. This Family is the best thing that happened and was given to me by my God. I’ll do my best to protect our own peace and live a happy life.

I was raised by my parents with my lola who guided me as well. During my childhood, I lived with my cousin (who become my best friend for life) and also with my uncles and aunties – we are living in one community, in one compound area. In my innocent mind we are happy but for my parents, living with relatives and staying for a longer time in anyone's house will create a problem. They infringe on your privacy and your freedom gets exploited.

After 3 years of living with them, my parents decided to buy our own house where we could live and build our own kingdom. But the house is not that far from our relatives (just a different barangay) where we are still connected and can still bond at sometimes.

My ancestors in both mother and father are all living originally from Bacolod, they are both Ilonggo and Ilongga. My Father’s clan (Milabo) is very well-known in the place where I grow up. But I am not that close to them because I grow up living with my Mother’s clan.

Our language is purely Hiligaynon but I am not used to it because I was born here in Manila specifically in Taguig. We just moved there (in Bacolod) when I was in my first grade till my High School Days and but still not used to speaking purely Hiligaynon. My comprehension was not that solid but I can understand and speak as well. Both my parents are going back in forth here in Manila working so they usually speak Tagalog inside our house.

My favorite thing to do when I was little are drawing and singing. But growing up, I lost those passions. Life hits me really hard growing up I develop depression and anxiety. But now with the help of my supportive husband, I feel gradually coming back to my own self.

My favorite food is anything salty, and just the right balance of sweet, salty, and spicy food. I don’t think of any specific food.

My favorite place to go is my house (Just staying in my house with my family). The church is the second place I always wanted to go to. Spending time outside is good as well as long as I have my family with me.

My favorite person is my lovely daughter. She’s 10x better than me. She’s the best version of me. I can’t thank God enough.
